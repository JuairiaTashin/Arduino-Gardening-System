Purpose:

The purpose of this gardening system is to make the various tasks that are involved in
gardening, such as watering, cutting, and monitoring plant health more efficient and easily
executable without the exertion of physical labor. Our robot ensures that gardening is super
easy and accessible to users.

Components:

● Arduino uno R3

● Relay mod 2 channel -80

● Servo motor SG90-2 ta-200

● battery 4+ battery holder-380

● Wire

● LCD Module with 12c interface: 16*2-230

● Breadboard

● 5v water pump

● Soil moisture sensor

● Temperature sensor

● Buck module-50

Future Enhancements:

The robot can be armed with advanced AI programmes to be able to adapt to changing
weather conditions. Better way of watering the lawn can be implemented. AI can also be
used to make the bot mow the lawn in certain patterns selected and controlled by the operator
through bluetooth or wifi. Bluetooth can be switched out for wifi with iot could connection,
for the bot to be more. Efficient. Additionally, it can be equipped with ultrasonic sensors or
lidars to detect obstacles such as rocks, debris, small animals and avoid them. Furthermore, it
can be programmed to clean the cut weed and for planting seeds as well. Also, it can be
integrated with smart home systems to be voice controlled handsfree. Finally, it can also be
equipped with an AI mapping feature to map and navigate through new terrain for better and
more efficient coverage.
