Components:
● Arduino uno R3
● Relay mod 2 channel -80
● Servo motor SG90-2 ta-200
● battery 4+ battery holder-380
● Wire
● LCD Module with 12c interface: 16*2-230
● Breadboard
● 5v water pump
● Soil moisture sensor
● Temperature sensor
● Buck module-50

Purpose
The purpose of this gardening system is to make the various tasks that are involved in
gardening, such as watering, cutting, and monitoring plant health more efficient and easily
executable without the exertion of physical labor. Our robot ensures that gardening is super
easy and accessible to users.
